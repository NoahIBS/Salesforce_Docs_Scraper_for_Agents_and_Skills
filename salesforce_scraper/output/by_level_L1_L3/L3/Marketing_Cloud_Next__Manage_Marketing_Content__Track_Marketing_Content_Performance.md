# Track Marketing Content Performance

Find out how users engage with your emails, SMS, WhatsApp messages, tracked links, and landing pages on the Content Performance dashboard. You can filter the data by date range, campaign, or flow. Track the success of different content variations among various audiences by monitoring unique clicks.

To access the Content Performance dashboard, open any content record and then select the **Performance** tab. For more information on available metrics, see [ Metric Formulas](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_reporting_formula_reference.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Reports and dashboards in Marketing Cloud Next contain a combination of direct and calculated values. The available metrics vary by channel and report location.").
