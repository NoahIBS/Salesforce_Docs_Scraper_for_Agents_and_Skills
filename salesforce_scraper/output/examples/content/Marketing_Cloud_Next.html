Marketing Cloud Next

Marketing Cloud Next brings the power of the platform to your team’s marketing efforts. You can integrate multiple data sources with Data 360, build content with Experience Cloud, power complex customer journeys with Flow Builder, and optimize with Agentforce along the way.

Required Editions

Available in: Salesforce Enterprise and Unlimited Editions with Marketing Cloud Next Growth or Advanced Edition

  • Manage Your Marketing App
    Salesforce admins, marketing admins, and admins for Data 360 have a role to play in configuring and managing Marketing Cloud Next. Before you get started, determine whether these one user fills these admin roles or if many people are involved.
  • Business Units in Marketing Cloud Next
    Business units create divisions within your org to focus on specific marketing goals, while also providing global visibility and control across the entire enterprise. Business units act as a junction between data and content, making sure that marketing efforts are aligned with specific organizational divisions.
  • Explore Agentforce and Einstein in Marketing Cloud Next
    Agentforce in Marketing Cloud Next helps you create campaigns, message content, landing pages, segments, and more. Einstein predictive features provide insights and data to inform the content and timing of your marketing activities.
  • Comply with Privacy Regulations
    Stay in compliance with privacy and consent regulations that apply to your business with consent management tools in Marketing Cloud Next. Use the default marketing communication subscription to collect and track consent data, or create your own custom subscriptions. Built-in preference pages make it easy for subscribers to manage their own communication preferences.
  • Target Audiences with Marketing Cloud Next
    The strongest campaigns target the right audience. After you determine your ideal audience member, you can reach them by filtering Data 360 data into a segment. Before you start using segments, make sure that a Data Cloud Architect or data analyst has configured incoming data streams and defined the reconciliation rules that manage harmonization across records.
  • Enhance Your Marketing with Marketing Cloud Next Campaigns
    Kick-start your marketing efforts with a centralized campaign hub where you can design and automate your content. In Marketing Cloud Next, each campaign starts with a flow. The flow automates content distribution and data collection.
  • Manage Content in Marketing Cloud Next
    Marketing Cloud Next provides the tools you need to create and store a variety of content to support your marketing efforts. The Content tab, powered by Salesforce CMS, is where you can organize your content in workspaces and folders. When you’re ready to create something new, create or clone and then edit a variety of marketing-specific content types in a consistent editing environment.
  • Measure Success in Marketing Cloud Next
    Monitoring performance and reporting on your successes are a significant part of a marketer’s job. Marketing Cloud Next includes reporting options powered by Data 360 and Tableau Next.
