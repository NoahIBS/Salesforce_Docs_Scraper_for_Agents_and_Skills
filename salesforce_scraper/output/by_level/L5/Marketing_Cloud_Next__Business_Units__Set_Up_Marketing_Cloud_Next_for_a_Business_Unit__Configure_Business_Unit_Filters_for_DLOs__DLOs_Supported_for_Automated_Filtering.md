# DLOs Supported for Automated Filtering

Automated filters are supported for several DLOs.

### Required Editions

Available in: Salesforce **Enterprise** and **Unlimited** Editions with Marketing Cloud Next **Advanced** Edition  
---  
  
  * ManagedContent
  * ManagedContentPublishedUrl
  * Campaign
  * Flow
    * FlowRecord
    * FlowRecordElement
    * FlowRecordVersion
    * FlowRecordVersionOccurrence
  * MarketSegment
  * BulkMessage
  * ListEmail
  * Personalization
    * AbnExperiment
    * AbnExperimentCohort
    * AbnExperimentationDailySummary
    * AbnExperimentationSummary
    * PersonalizationDecision
    * PersonalizationPoint
    * PersonalizationRecommender
    * PersonalizationSchema
  * Engagement
    * MessagingEventsSmsV2
    * MessagingEventsEmailV2
    * MessagingEventsWhatsAppV2
  * Instrumentation
    * BEHAVIORAL_EVENTS
    * CONTACT_POINT_ADDRESS
    * CONTACT_POINT_EMAIL
    * CONTACT_POINT_PHONE
    * IDENTITY
    * PARTY_IDENTIFICATION
