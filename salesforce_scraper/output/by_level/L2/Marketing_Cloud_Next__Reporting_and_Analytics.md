# Measure Success in Marketing Cloud Next

Monitoring performance and reporting on your successes are a significant part of a marketer’s job. Marketing Cloud Next includes reporting options powered by Data 360 and Tableau Next.

### Required Editions

Available in: Salesforce **Enterprise** and **Unlimited** Editions with Marketing Cloud Next **Growth** or **Advanced** Edition  
---  
  
  * **[Reporting in Marketing Cloud Next](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_reporting_types.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
After you set up Marketing Performance and install the required analytics packages, you get access to a number of different reporting options in Marketing Cloud Next.
  * **[Customize an Analytics Dashboard in Marketing Cloud Next](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_reporting_create.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
Analytics dashboards are located on the Analytics tab and powered by Data 360. You can use the dashboards as-is or customize them to meet your business needs. Analytics dashboards are created from a group of report widgets. You can rearrange the widgets, change labels, filter data, and more.
  * **[Metrics Formulas for Marketing Cloud Next](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_reporting_formula_reference.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
Reports and dashboards in Marketing Cloud Next contain a combination of direct and calculated values. The available metrics vary by channel and report location.
