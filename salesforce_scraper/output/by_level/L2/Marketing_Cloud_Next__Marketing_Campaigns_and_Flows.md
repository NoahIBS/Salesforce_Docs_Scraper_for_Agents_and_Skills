# Enhance Your Marketing with Marketing Cloud Next Campaigns

Kick-start your marketing efforts with a centralized campaign hub where you can design and automate your content. In Marketing Cloud Next, each campaign starts with a flow. The flow automates content distribution and data collection.

### Required Editions

Available in: Salesforce **Enterprise** and **Unlimited** Editions with Marketing Cloud Next **Growth** or **Advanced** Edition, and in **Starter**  and **Pro Suite**  Editions. Your edition determines the options that you have.  
---  
  
When you create a campaign, you can select a Quick Start option that comes with a preconfigured flow or select Build Your Own to create a campaign and flow from the ground up. Campaign options can help you get started faster, whether you’re creating one piece of content or a multistep customer experience.

  * **[Understanding Campaigns and Flows in Marketing Cloud Next](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_campaigns_flows_concepts.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
Campaigns and flows are two distinct objects, but they work together to organize and execute your marketing work in Marketing Cloud Next. The campaign record aims to simplify how you connect the assets and metadata that comprise a marketing effort. A flow is the engine that prepares and executes the campaign.
  * **[Create and Manage a Campaign Flow in Marketing Cloud Next](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_campaigns_working.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
You can create a campaign flow from any campaign Quick Start or from a blank template, and then add flow elements that listen for activities and trigger actions.
  * **[Manage Campaigns and Flows with Marketing Calendar](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_track_campaigns_with_calendar.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
Track and manage your upcoming or ongoing campaigns and segment flows effortlessly from the Marketing Calendar.
  * **[Marketing Cloud Engagement Journeys and Campaigns](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_campaigns_journeys.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
Use the campaign object as a shared workspace across Marketing Cloud Engagement and Marketing Cloud Next. Associate journeys to a campaign to streamline workflows and facilitate in-depth reporting.
