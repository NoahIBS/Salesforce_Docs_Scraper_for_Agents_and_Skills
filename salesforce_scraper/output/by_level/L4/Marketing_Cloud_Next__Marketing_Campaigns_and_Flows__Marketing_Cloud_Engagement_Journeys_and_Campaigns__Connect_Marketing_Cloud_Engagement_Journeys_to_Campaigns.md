# Connect Marketing Cloud Engagement Journeys to Campaigns

Organize journeys and enhance reporting by linking Marketing Cloud Engagement journeys to Campaigns. Connecting journeys to a Campaign helps group them into a larger marketing effort. It also automatically creates a grouped view of their marketing performance.

### Required Editions

Available in: Salesforce **Enterprise** and **Unlimited** Editions with Marketing Cloud Next **Growth** or **Advanced** Edition  
---  
User Permissions Needed  
---  
To manage and edit Campaigns: | Marketing Cloud Manager permission set, Marketing Cloud AdminORView Flows and Create and Edit Flows user permissions AND Permissions to all elements in the flow  
  
[Set up reporting in Marketing Cloud Next. ](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_admin_setup_reporting.htm&language=en_US&type=5)

Note

In Journey Builder, connected journeys display an icon that indicates they’re associated with a Campaign.

  1. From the Campaigns tab, create a campaign.
  2. Name your campaign, enter the details that you need, and click **Save**.
  3. Click **+** next to Journeys on the Campaign page.
  4. Select your journeys in Marketing Cloud Engagement.
     * The journey can't already be connected to a Campaign.
     * A journey can be connected to only one Campaign.
     * Journeys must be in Draft, Running, Finishing, Paused, or Stopped mode.
     * There's a limit of 20 associated journeys per Campaign.
  5. Click **Add**.
