# Send an Email with an Approved Distributed Marketing Template

After a marketing user activates an event-triggered flow with an approved email template, the email template becomes available to use with Distributed Marketing and Alerts. 

### Required Editions

Available in: Salesforce **Enterprise** and **Unlimited** Editions with Marketing Cloud Next **Growth** or **Advanced** Edition  
---  
User Permissions Needed  
---  
To send a Distributed Messages and Alerts Email | Distributed Marketing and Alerts User permission setANDMarketing Cloud Manager permission set  
  |    
  
  1. From a person record or list view, open the email composer.
     1. From lead, contact, or prospect record, in the Distributed Messages component, click **Send Email Messages**.
     2. From a lead, contact, or prospect list view, click **Send Distributed Message**.
     3. From a campaign members related list, select campaign members. Then, click the dropdown menu and select **Send Distributed Message**.
  2. Select the campaign and flow that includes the template you want to use.
  3. Edit your email content, and then click Continue.
  4. Preview your email and enter a From address and reply-to address.
  5. When you’re done, send your message.
