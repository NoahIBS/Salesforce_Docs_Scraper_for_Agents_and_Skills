# Working with Segments from a Campaign

Marketing Cloud Next offers options for working with segments from a campaign record. When working with a segment-triggered campaign flow, you can create a segment or use an existing one. You can also preview any segment to get an idea of who will receive your messages.

### Required Editions

Available in: Salesforce **Enterprise** and **Unlimited** Editions with Marketing Cloud Next **Growth** or **Advanced** Edition, and in **Starter**  and **Pro Suite**  Editions. Your edition determines the options that you have.  
---  
  
## Segment Options

Here are the options for creating a segment from the campaign record.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

  * Use Quick Filters—Apply commonly used attribute filters to create a targeted segment.
  * Send to Campaign Members—Create a segment for you based on campaign membership. For more on campaign members, see [Work with Campaign Members](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=sales.campaigns_members_working_with_parent.htm&language=en_US&type=5).
  * Go to Segment Builder—Manually create a segment in the segment canvas.



[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Previewing a Segment

You can preview segment membership for any published segment that you add to a campaign flow. To use the preview feature and send messages, you must publish your segment. If your segment hasn’t been published recently, we recommend you publish it again so you’re previewing the most up-to-date population.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

The segment preview shows up to 1,000 members of your segment. You can also search for specific people based on First Name, Last Name, or Unified Individual ID.
