# Working with Prospects

Focus on high-value prospects, while unqualified ones remain hidden until they reach the required engagement score. You can add prospects to Marketing Cloud Next manually or import them in bulk.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Add a Prospect

You can add new prospects if you have potential buyers who are yet to be qualified as leads.

  1. From the **Prospects** tab, click **New**.
  2. Enter the prospect's information. Provide at least last name, email, or phone number.
  3. Click **Save**.



[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Import Prospects

If you want to add multiple prospects, you can import them using a CSV file.

  1. From the **Prospects** tab, click **Import**.
  2. Click **Import from File** and click **Next**.
  3. Click **Upload File** , select the CSV file, and click **Next**.
  4. Map each column from your file to the Salesforce field, and click **Start Import**.

Make sure that the **Consent Status** column includes opt-in values if you plan to send marketing emails.

  5. Click **Done**.



[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Create a Unified Individual Segment for Prospects

Prospects are treated as unified individuals, so you can create a unified individual segment, define rules for your prospects, and add them to campaigns. 

For more information, see [Manually Create a Segment](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_audiences_segment_create_manual.htm&language=en_US&type=5) and [Segment Your Data with Attributes](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=data.c360_a_segmentation_interface.htm&language=en_US&type=5).
