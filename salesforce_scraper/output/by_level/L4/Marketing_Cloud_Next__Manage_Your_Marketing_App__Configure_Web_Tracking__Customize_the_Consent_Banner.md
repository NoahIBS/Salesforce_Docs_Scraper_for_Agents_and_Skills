# Customize the Consent Banner

To protect the privacy of visitors to your Salesforce-hosted marketing landing pages, as well as your external sites, use the default consent banner to collect consent to track. You’re responsible for complying with privacy laws and regulations applicable to your business. 

### Required Editions

Available in: Salesforce **Enterprise** and **Unlimited** Editions with Marketing Cloud Next **Growth** or **Advanced** Edition  
---  
User Permissions Needed  
---  
To customize the consent banner: | Marketing Cloud Admin permission set  
  
  1. From Setup, in the Quick Find box, enter `Web Tracking`, and then select **Consent Banner**. 
  2. Use the style fields to customize the banner’s position, fonts, and colors. 
  3. Edit the default text and enter a link to your company privacy policy. 
  4. Save your changes.
  5. Follow the steps to add the banner to your [landing page](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_tracking_landing_pages_parent.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Track visitor activities on your Marketing Cloud Next landing pages. To protect the privacy of your landing page visitors, use the default consent banner to collect consent to track. You’re responsible for complying with privacy laws and regulations applicable to your business.") or [external site](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_consent_tracking_external.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Track visitor activities on web pages hosted outside of Salesforce, such as your company website. To protect the privacy of your site visitors, use the default consent banner to collect consent to track. You’re responsible for complying with privacy laws and regulations applicable to your business."). 



If you change the banner after publishing, publish the banner again in Experience Cloud. To test the banner on a real page, create and view a landing page in a web browser.
