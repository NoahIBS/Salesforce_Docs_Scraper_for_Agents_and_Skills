# Distributed Marketing and Alerts

To increase productivity and maintain brand consistency, marketers can share approved email templates with sales reps and other Salesforce users. Distributed Marketing and Alerts makes it easier for non-marketing users to send email content that’s on brand and approved by marketing.

### Required Editions

Available in: Salesforce **Enterprise** and **Unlimited** Editions with Marketing Cloud Next **Growth** or **Advanced** Edition  
---  
  
  * **[Configure an Approved Email Template for Non-Marketing Users](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_content_dma_create_message.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
After an admin enables Distributed Marketing and Alerts, designate a marketing-approved email template for non-marketing users to use to send email. First, create the template in Salesforce CMS. Then, create an event-triggered campaign and select the template in the flow.
  * **[Send an Email with an Approved Distributed Marketing Template](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_content_dm_send_message.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
After a marketing user activates an event-triggered flow with an approved email template, the email template becomes available to use with Distributed Marketing and Alerts.
