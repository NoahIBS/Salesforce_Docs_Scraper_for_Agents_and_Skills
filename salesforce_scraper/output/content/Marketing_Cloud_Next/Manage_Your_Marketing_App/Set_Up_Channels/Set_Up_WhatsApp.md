# Set Up WhatsApp in Marketing Cloud Next

Reach a global audience with personalized conversational messages that can include text, audio, video, and other rich content.

### Required Editions

Salesforce **Enterprise** and **Unlimited** Editions with Marketing Cloud Next **Growth** or **Advanced** Edition and the Salesforce Message Credits - WhatsApp add-on  
---  
  
Note WhatsApp messaging isn't supported in Government Cloud. See [Marketing Cloud Next in Government Cloud](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.government_cloud_marketing_cloud.htm&language=en_US&type=5).

  1. From Setup, in the Quick Find box, enter `Your`, and then select **Your Numbers**.
  2. Install the required managed packages for consent and engagement. See [Manage WhatsApp Consent in Unified Messaging](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.um_consent_whatsapp.htm&language=en_US&type=5). 
  3. Create or connect a WhatsApp Business Account. See [Connect Your WhatsApp Business Account](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.um_channel_whatsapp_connect_waba.htm&language=en_US&type=5).
  4. Review or update your consent validation settings. 
     1. From Setup, in the Quick Find box, enter `Channels`, and then select **WhatsApp**.
     2. Choose whether to check consent on a communication subscription before sending promotional and transactional messages. 



After you connect your WhatsApp Business Account to Salesforce, you can: 

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

  * [Create communication subscriptions](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_consent_comm_sub_create.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Create a communication subscription for each category of marketing content that you send, such as product updates. For each subscription that you create, add marketing channels, such as email and SMS. Subscribers must give consent to receive promotional messages from each channel type.") to store consent for WhatsApp messages.
  * [Import consent data](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_consent_import_create.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "If you collect consent data outside of Marketing Cloud Next, import it as a CSV. Each import corresponds to a single marketing channel, communication subscription, and consent status.").
  * [Set up Marketing Performance](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=mktg.mktg_admin_marketing_performance.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Marketing Performance uses Data 360 and Tableau Next to present detailed metrics about your campaigns and content. Get embedded reporting in your campaign records, and access a comprehensive dashboard with insights and trends about your overall marketing efforts.") to get metrics about your new channel. 
  * Begin crafting WhatsApp campaign content.
