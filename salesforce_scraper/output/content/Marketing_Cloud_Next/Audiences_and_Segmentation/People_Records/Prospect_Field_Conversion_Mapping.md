# Prospect Field Conversion Mapping 

To ensure accurate data transfer, see how the prospect’s fields map to the fields in the Lead, Contact, or Account on conversion.

Prospect Field | Description | Maps to  
---|---|---  
Annual Revenue | The prospect company's yearly revenue. | Lead: Annual RevenueContact: N/AAccount: Annual Revenue  
City | City that’s included in the prospect’s address. Up to 40 characters allowed. | Lead: CityContact: Mailing CityAccount: Billing City  
Company | The prospect’s company name Up to 255 characters allowed. | Lead: CompanyContact: Account NameAccount: Account Name  
Country | Country that’s included in the prospect’s address. Entry is selected from a picklist of standard values or entered as text. Up to 80 characters allowed. | Lead: CountryContact: Mailing CountryAccount: Mailing Country  
Created By | The user who created the prospect record. | -  
Description | Description of the prospect. Up to 32 KB of data is allowed. Only the first 255 characters appear in reports. | Lead: DescriptionContact: DescriptionAccount: N/A  
Email | The prospect’s email address. Up to 80 characters allowed. | Lead: EmailContact: EmailAccount: N/A  
First Name | The prospect’s first name. Up to 40 characters allowed. | Lead: First NameContact: First NameAccount: N/A  
Gender | The prospect’s self-identified experience of their gender, which does or doesn’t correspond to the prospect’s designated sex at birth. | Lead: GenderContact: GenderAccount: N/A  
Industry | Primary business of the prospect’s company. Entry is selected from a picklist of available values, which are set by an admin. Up to 40 characters allowed. | Lead: IndustryContact: N/AAccount: Industry  
Last Name | The prospect’s last name. Up to 80 characters allowed. | Lead: Last NameContact: Last NameAccount: N/A  
Lead Source | Source of lead, for example, Advertisement, Partner, or Web. Entry is selected from a picklist of available values, which are set by an admin. Up to 40 characters allowed. | Lead: Lead SourceContact: Lead SourceAccount: N/A  
Modified By | The user who last changed the prospect fields, including the modification date and time.  | -  
Name | The combined first and last name of prospect, as displayed on prospect's detail page. | Lead: NameContact: NameAccount: N/A  
No. of Employees | The number of employees at the prospect’s company. | Lead: No. of EmployeesContact: NAAccount: No. of Employees  
Phone | The prospect’s primary phone number. Up to 40 characters allowed. | Lead: PhoneContact: PhoneAccount: Phone  
Prospect Currency | The prospect’s preferred currency. | Lead: Lead CurrencyContact: Contact CurrencyAccount: Account Currency  
Prospect Record Name | The unique ID for each prospect record. | -  
Prospect Status | The business lifecycle stage that best describes the prospect’s journey. | -  
Salutation | The prospect’s preferred title. For example, Mr., Ms., Dr., or Prof. Entry is selected from a picklist of available values, which are set by an admin. Up to 40 characters allowed. | Lead: SalutationContact: SalutationAccount: N/A  
State/Province | State or province that’s included in the prospect’s address. Entry is selected from a picklist of standard values, or entered as text. Up to 80 characters allowed. | Lead: State/ ProvinceContact: Mailing State/ ProvinceAccount: Billing State/Province  
Title | The prospect’s business title. Up to 128 characters are allowed. When converting a Prospect to a lead or contact, the conversion fails if the lead's Title field contains more than 80 characters. | Lead: TitleContact: TitleAccount: N/A  
Zip/Postal Code | ZIP code or postal code that’s included in the prospect’s address. Up to 20 characters allowed. | Lead: Zip/Postal CodeContact: Mailing Zip/Postal CodeAccount: Billing Zip/Postal Code
