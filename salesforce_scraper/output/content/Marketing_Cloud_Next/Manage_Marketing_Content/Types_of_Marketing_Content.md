# Types of Content in Marketing Cloud Next

Create and manage all your marketing content from your CMS workspace. You can create content such as emails, landing pages, forms, images, and SMS messages for use in marketing campaigns.

### Required Editions

Available in: Salesforce **Enterprise** and **Unlimited** Editions with Marketing Cloud Next **Growth** or **Advanced** Edition, and in **Starter**  and **Pro Suite**  Editions. Your edition determines the options that you have.  
---  
  
Type | Description | Good For... | Available In...  
---|---|---|---  
Brand | Specify your company’s unique colors, text styles, and more to apply a consistent look and feel across all your marketing content. | Ensuring that all your marketing content is on brand. You can also create separate brands for specific events and products.Available in emails, forms, and landing pages. | These content types: 

  * Email
  * Landing pages
  * Form

  
Email | Create promotional or transactional emails by adding and editing familiar components. | Promoting products, services, or events, or for sharing updates about a transaction. | These campaign types: 

  * Single email
  * Message series
  * Blank campaign

  
Expression | Save filter and sort criteria for selecting a data attribute for a merge field that you can use in your marketing messages. | Reducing message design time and ensuring consistency by reusing merge field definitions across marketing content and controlling how data is referenced.  | These message types: 

  * Email
  * SMS
  * WhatsApp

  
Form | Create forms that collect user information. | Capturing user information to generate leads. | These campaign types: 

  * Signup form

  
Image | Upload a media file or link to a trusted external URL. You can add a BMP, GIF, PNG, JPG, or JPEG file up to 25 MB. For WhatsApp messages, you can add a JPEG or PNG file up to 5 MB.See [Manage Trusted URLs](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=xcloud.security_trusted_urls_manage.htm&language=en_US&type=5). | Enhancing emails, landing pages, or forms with logos, product photos, graphics, or other visuals. | These campaign and content types: 

  * Single email
  * Message series
  * Signup form
  * Blank email
  * WhatsApp

Images aren’t supported in SMS messages.  
Landing Page | Create promotional pages for campaign content or calls to action. | Sharing introductory information and hosting forms to gather prospect details for lead generation efforts. | These campaign types: 

  * Signup from

  
Push Notification Message | Create push notification messages for users. | Engaging with users directly on their mobile devices to facilitate real-time marketing. | These campaign flow types:

  * Segment triggered
  * Event triggered

You must add the Send Mobile App Message element manually for basic and carousel push types.  
Reusable Content Block | Create reusable banners, footers, or other boilerplate blocks of content and use them across multiple emails. | Speeding up email production, maintaining consistency, boosting personalization | This content type: 

  * Email 

  
SMS Message | Available with the Salesforce Message Credits - SMS add-on.Create text-only marketing content that can be sent from short or long SMS codes. | Sharing updates about an ongoing transaction or for sending short promotional messages. | These campaign flow types:

  * Segment triggered

You must add the Send SMS Message element manually.  
Tracked link | Add a link to drive traffic to your external website from any content and collect engagement data. | Determining which sources result in the most engagement and site traffic. | Can be added manually to these content types:

  * Email
  * SMS
  * WhatsApp
  * Landing pages
  * External content, such as an ad

  
WhatsApp | Create promotional and transactional use cases that include personalization, interactive elements, and rich media files. | Promoting products, services, or events, or for sharing updates about a transaction. | These campaign flow types:[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

  * Segment triggered
  * Event triggered

You must add the Send WhatsApp Message element manually.  
  
These content types are available only if your account has the WhatsApp add-on.
